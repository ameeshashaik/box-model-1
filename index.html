Grapes

Juicy and sweet, grapes are rich in antioxidants and perfect as a refreshing snack. They are often used in juices, and fruit salads.

Apple

Apples are crisp and mildly sweet fruits packed with fiber and vitamins. A popular choice for healthy snacking and baking.

Kiwi

Kiwi is a tangy and sweet fruit with vibrant green flesh and tiny black seeds. It is rich in Vitamin C and boosts immunity.

Pineapple

Pineapples are tropical fruits known for their bold, sweet-tart flavor. They aid digestion and are often used in juices and desserts.
